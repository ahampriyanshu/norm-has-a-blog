## Writing Posts

To create a new post, simply add a new `.md` file to the `src/posts` directory. Each post should include frontmatter with metadata:

```markdown
---
title: Your Post Title
description: A brief description of your post
date: '2024-01-15'
tags:
  - tag1
  - tag2
pin: false
---

Your content here...
\```
